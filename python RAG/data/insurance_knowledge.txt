Health insurance covers medical expenses such as hospitalization,
doctor visits, medicines, and preventive care.

Premium is the amount a customer pays regularly to keep an
insurance policy active.

Deductible is the amount a policyholder must pay before the
insurance company starts covering costs.

Higher deductibles usually result in lower premiums.

Young drivers under 25 often have higher car insurance premiums
because of higher accident risk.

Life insurance provides financial protection to family members
after the policyholder’s death.
